Good to know

Common questions

Where are The Barham Lakes?

On Pesthouse Lane, Barham, near Ipswich, Suffolk, IP6 0PF — 31 acres of otter-fenced water across four lakes and a private river. Viewing is by appointment only.

Can I hire a lake exclusively and stay in the lodge?

Yes — Brook Pool is available for exclusive hire together with the lakeside lodge, which sleeps up to eight anglers. Stays are booked online and secured with a 30% deposit.

How much does exclusive lake & lodge hire cost?

Priced by season and length of stay — typically from around £480 for a short off-peak stay, up to peak and festive rates. Final pricing is confirmed at booking. See live availability on the booking page.

How do I join the syndicate?

Apply online for a Specimen Carp, Specimen Pike or Complete Complex ticket. The Complete Complex ticket gives access to every lake and the private river, all year round.

What fish can you catch?

Specimen carp into the 40s and 50s, plus pike and rare crucian carp, across mature gravel pits with islands, bars and bays.

Exclusive carp & pike fishing and a luxury lakeside lodge in the heart of Suffolk. Viewing by appointment only.
