About The Barham Lakes

Specimen fishing designed by anglers, for anglers — in the heart of Suffolk.

Our story

A complex built for the specimen angler

The Barham Lakes is a private angling complex just outside Ipswich, set within over 3km of otter fencing and daily site management — more than 31 acres of water across four mature gravel-pit lakes and 1,000 metres of private river on the Gipping.

Since 2018 we have managed the waters for one thing: quality specimen fishing. Old Gipping-valley carp in excess of 30lb sit alongside a careful stocking programme, with genuine 40s and 50s among the targets, plus pike and rare crucian carp across the syndicate waters.

New for 2026, a luxury lakeside lodge on Brook Pool means you can now fish exclusively and stay on the bank — the whole lake, and the lodge, to yourself.
